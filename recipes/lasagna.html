Lasagna

Description

Feeling Italian? Try making this world famous lasagna!

The melted cheese in conjunction with the slow cooked ragu and bechamel sauce makes this an unforgettable classic!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Prepare the ragu
  2. Prepare the bechamel
  3. Put it all together
  4. Cook in the oven
